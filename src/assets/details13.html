Gulmohar Tree (Delonix regia)

Scientific Name: Delonix regia

Description: Gulmohar, also called the Flame Tree or Royal Poinciana, is known for its large, vibrant red-orange flowers. It's a fast-growing deciduous tree with a wide-spreading canopy, making it a popular ornamental tree in tropical regions. The fern-like leaves add a soft texture to its striking floral display.

Care Tips:

  • Water: Regular watering when young; drought-tolerant once mature.
  • Light: Needs full sun for best flowering.
  • Temperature: Thrives in warm climates above 20Â°C.
  • Soil: Prefers well-drained soils; tolerant of poor soil conditions.

Medicinal Uses:

Traditionally, parts of the Gulmohar tree are used in folk medicine for treating inflammation and pain. Its flowers and leaves are also explored for their antioxidant properties.
